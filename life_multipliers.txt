# Life Insurance Quoting Rules (Maryland)

## Base Rates (10-Year Term, $100,000 Coverage)
Monthly Premium by Age:

Age 5 = $8
Age 10 = $9
Age 15 = $10
Age 18 = $12
Age 25 = $14
Age 30 = $16
Age 35 = $18
Age 40 = $22
Age 45 = $30
Age 50 = $42
Age 55 = $60
Age 59 = $75
Age 65 = $115

If exact age not listed, choose closest reasonable age.

---

## Term Multipliers

10-year term = 1.0
20-year term = 1.6
30-year term = 2.2

---

## Coverage Multipliers

$50,000 = 0.6
$100,000 = 1.0
$250,000 = 2.2
$500,000 = 4.1

---

## Health Multipliers

Excellent = 0.85
Good = 1.0
Average = 1.3
Below Average = 1.7

---

## Tobacco Multipliers

Non-smoker = 1.0
Smoker = 2.3

---

## Calculation Formula

Final Monthly Premium =
Base Rate (by age)
× Term Multiplier
× Coverage Multiplier
× Health Multiplier
× Tobacco Multiplier
